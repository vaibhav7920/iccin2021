Call for Papers
On behalf of the Organizing Committee, Deparment of Computer Science , Bhagwan Parshuram Institute of Technology is organizing its 4th International Conference on Computing (ICCIN-2021).

ICCIN-2021 accepts regular papers of 6-8 pages including figures and references. The submitted papers will be peer-reviewed by three reviewers out of which two positive reports will entitle a paper to be accepted in ICCIN-2021. At-least one author registration is required after the paper has been accepted. Papers not presented at the conference will not be included in the conference proceedings.

A great opportunity for the authors to publish their work in two different series ICCIN-2021 ESCI series or ICCIN-202 SPRINGER series or both one desk.
ESCI Series
All Selected papers will be published in ESCI Journal (Taylor & Francis):
Journal of Information & Optimization Sciences (JIOS) (ISSN: 0252-2667) and
Journal of Statistics & Management Systems (JSMS) (ISSN: 0972-0510).
Springer Series
All Selected papers will be submitted for publication in Springer LNNS Series, Scopus Indexed
Prospective authors are invited to submit high-quality papers representing original results in the emerging areas of computing and communications.
