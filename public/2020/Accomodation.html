Accommodation
Delhi have good and convenient accommodation options for visitors. You can book the accommodation prior to visit to avoid any problems. There is an array of hotels in Delhi with a range of price to suit your needs.

Few of the nearest hotels are:
  • Lemon Tree Premier Delhi - Business Hotel in Delhi Aerocity
  • Crowne Plaza - Rohini, New Delhi
  • Hotel Sewa Grand - Pitampura, New Delhi
  • Caspia Hotel - Shalimar Bagh, New Delhi
  • Hotel City Park - Pitampura, New Delhi
