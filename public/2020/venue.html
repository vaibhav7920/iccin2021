Getting to Vivanta
Delhi is well connected by road, rail and air.

Flying in:
The nearest airport is Indira Gandhi International Airport which is one hour drive by road. The airport serves as a hub for AirAsia India, Alliance Air, Jet Airways, IndiGo, Air India and a focus city for SpiceJet. There are direct international flights to Delhi from Kuala Lumpur, Bhubaneswar, Sharjah, Paris, Singapore, London, Hong-Kong, Dubai, Frankfurt, Muscat, Bangkok etc... Prepaid taxis are available at the Indira Gandhi International airport.
Also one can take metro (Airport Line) from airport.

By road:
Delhi is well connected by road network. One can take a cab and reach to Vivanta in Dwarka or cheaper mode of transport can be Delhi Transport Corporation (DTC) buses on route 718.

By train:
The nearest railway station is New Delhi Railway Station (NDLS) from which one can take Airport Line by walkig 500 meters.

By metro:
The nearest metro station is Dwarka Sector-21 on Airport Line and Dwarka Sector-8 on Blue Line.
